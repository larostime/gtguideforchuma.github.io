Guide how to enter information in GetterTools

Go to the plate with the troops in Travian

Copy all informations about troops (what you need is highlighted with a red rectangle)

Step 2

Now go to GetterTools and choose "Trooptool"

Step 3

Go to your troops and scroll down to analizator
